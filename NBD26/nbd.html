Social Initiative

IISc Notebook Drive 2026

I had the privilege of volunteering for the IISc Notebook Drive, a student-run social welfare initiative at the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. The campaign aims to support the education of children in underfunded government schools by providing them with essential learning resources.

During the drive, we successfully distributed over 2,000 notebooks and academic supplies to students in need. Working alongside fellow students to organize the logistics, package the materials, and interact with the children during the distribution was an incredibly rewarding experience. Supporting these bright young minds and encouraging their educational journeys remains one of my most cherished experiences at IISc.
