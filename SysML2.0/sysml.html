Systems for ML Workshop 2.0

I had the incredible opportunity to volunteer at the 2nd edition of the Systems for Machine Learning Workshop, organized by the IISc ACM and ACM-W Student Chapters from June 26th to 28th, 2026. The event brought together leading researchers and practitioners to explore the critical intersection of machine learning and systems design.

Throughout the three days, I assisted in coordinating various sessions and ensuring the smooth execution of the agenda. It was a fantastic experience connecting with experts and learning about cutting-edge developments such as Systems Profiling, Distributed Machine Learning, and Agentic Edge AI.
